Community Guidelines

These Community Guidelines and our Terms of Service are there to ensure every Emarat-Al-Aman user has a safe and enjoyable experience on Emarat-Al-Aman. Failure to follow these guidelines may result in your account being suspended and you losing access to Emarat-Al-Aman.

Respect others. Treat them as you would want to be treated. Do not engage in any form of hate speech, rude or abusive behaviour, bullying, or misogyny.

Respect and Courtesy:

Treat all members of the Emarat Al-Aman community with respect and courtesy.

Avoid using offensive language, harassment, or personal attacks in any form.

Educational Environment:

Emarat Al-Aman is a platform dedicated to cybersecurity education.

Stay focused on cybersecurity-related discussions, courses, and activities.

Avoid off-topic discussions that do not contribute to the learning environment.

Supportive Environment:

Foster a supportive environment where users feel comfortable asking questions, seeking guidance, and sharing their experiences related to cybersecurity.

Help others learn and grow by offering assistance and encouragement.

Compliance with Laws and Regulations:

Ensure that all interactions and contributions on the Emarat Al-Aman platform comply with applicable laws and regulations related to cybersecurity, intellectual property, and online conduct.

Community Engagement:

Stay actively engaged with the Emarat Al-Aman community by participating in discussions, courses, quizzes, and other activities.

Share your knowledge and expertise to contribute to the collective learning experience.



By following these community guidelines, we can create a positive and enriching environment for cybersecurity education and empowerment within the Emarat Al-Aman community. Thank you for your cooperation and commitment to cybersecurity excellence.
