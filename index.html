Why Emarat Al-Aman?

Emarat Al-Aman is a mobile application dedicated to educating users about cybersecurity in an engaging and interactive manner. Our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills necessary to protect themselves and their digital assets in an increasingly connected world.

E-Advisor

Have questions about cybersecurity? Chat with our virtual assistant powered by Googles Gemini to get instant answers and guidance on cybersecurity-related queries.

Cyber Watch

Explore a variety of cybersecurity courses covering topics such as malware detection, data encryption, phishing awareness, and more. Our courses are designed by cybersecurity experts and tailored to users of all skill levels.

QuizUp

Test your cybersecurity knowledge and skills with our quiz game and Pictionary game. Challenge yourself and compete with friends to see who can achieve the highest scores.

Know It & Pick It

Scenario based images that test your cyber security knowledge with real time security scenario.
